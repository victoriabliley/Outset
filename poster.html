The Problem

College students and young adults face too many barriers when trying to spend time outside and be active.

  • 🔍 Hard to find activities
  • ⏰ Difficult to schedule
  • ❓ Lack of information
  • 📱 No centralized platform

Our Solution

The Outset website makes discovering outdoor activities easy and streamlined.

  • 🌍 Search by location
  • 🎯 Filter by preferences
  • 📅 Schedule activities
  • ☀️ Weather-aware recommendations
  • 🗺️ Direct navigation
