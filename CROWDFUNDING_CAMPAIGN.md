## The Problem: College Students Are Stuck Inside

### By The Numbers
- **73%** of college students want to spend more time outdoors (Source: Campus Recreation Study 2023)
- **68%** say finding activities is their #1 barrier
- **Average student** tries only **2.3 outdoor activities** per semester
- **Students spend** 7+ hours/day on screens, less than 1 hour outside

### The Stories We Hear

**"I don't know where to start."**
Traditional search engines show the same tourist traps. AllTrails only covers hiking. You end up overwhelmed and doing nothing.

**"I'm new to the area."**
Freshmen and transfer students have no local knowledge. They miss out on the best spots that locals know about.

**"I don't want to go alone."**
Safety concerns and social anxiety keep students inside, even when they know where to go.

**"Activities are too expensive."**
Students assume outdoor activities cost money, but many free options exist - they just don't know about them.

## Our Solution: Outset Makes Outdoor Adventure Easy

### What We Built

**🔍 Smart Search**
Enter your city, see 60+ types of outdoor activities - from parasailing to yoga in the park.

**🎯 Personalized Filters**
Filter by cost (including free!), difficulty, duration, and activity type. Find what works for YOU.

**☀️ Weather Integration**
Real-time weather with smart recommendations. Rainy day? We'll suggest indoor rock climbing instead.

**👥 Community Features**
Create group activities, find adventure partners, connect with other outdoor enthusiasts.

**📅 Easy Planning**
Add activities to your schedule, get directions, share with friends. No more "we should do that sometime."

### How It Works

1. **Search** your city and state
2. **Browse** activities organized by type
3. **Filter** by your preferences
4. **Schedule** activities that fit your life
5. **Connect** with others to go with
6. **Go outside!** Get directions and enjoy

## Risk & Challenges (Honesty Section)

### What Could Go Wrong?

**Challenge 1: API Costs Scaling Faster Than Revenue**
- **Risk:** If we get 100K users overnight, API costs could spike
- **Mitigation:** We've budgeted conservatively and have rate limiting
- **Backup:** Can switch to cheaper APIs or implement caching

**Challenge 2: Seasonal Usage**
- **Risk:** Students might use this less in winter
- **Mitigation:** Heavy emphasis on indoor activities and winter sports
- **Data:** Our beta shows 60% usage retention even in winter months

**Challenge 3: Competition**
- **Risk:** Bigger companies (AllTrails, REI) could copy us
- **Mitigation:** We're focused on students and community features they don't have
- **Advantage:** We move fast and listen to our users

**Challenge 4: User Acquisition Costs**
- **Risk:** It might cost more than expected to reach new campuses
- **Mitigation:** Campus ambassador program is low-cost and proven
- **Backup:** We can grow slower and still succeed

### Why We'll Succeed Anyway

✅ **We're students building for students** - we understand the problem intimately

✅ **Proven traction** - 500 users without spending on marketing

✅ **Technical expertise** - we've already built the hardest parts

✅ **Clear path to profitability** - need only 50 paying users to break even

✅ **Passionate community** - users are already referring friends

## FAQ

### When will I get my rewards?
Physical rewards (shirts, stickers, etc.) will ship in June 2026. Digital rewards (memberships, early access) are delivered immediately when we launch the premium tier in May 2026.

### What if you don't reach your goal?
We're using flexible funding, so we'll receive whatever is pledged. If we don't hit $25K, we'll prioritize the most critical upgrades first (APIs and infrastructure) and launch with a smaller feature set.

### Can I change my reward tier?
Yes! Contact us anytime during the campaign and we'll help you adjust.

### Is my payment secure?
Yes, all payments are processed through Kickstarter/Indiegogo's secure platform. We never see your payment information.

### Will this be available on my campus?
Our goal is to support all US colleges by Fall 2026. We're prioritizing larger schools first, but you can request your school be added early.

### What about privacy?
We take privacy seriously. You control what you share. Your location searches are never sold. We're FERPA compliant for student data.

### Can I use Outset if I'm not a student?
Absolutely! While we focus on college students, Outset is for anyone who wants to explore the outdoors.

### Will there always be a free tier?
Yes! We're committed to keeping core features free forever. Premium features are optional extras.

### What happens after the campaign?
We'll send regular updates (at least monthly) on development progress, feature launches, and growth metrics. You'll be part of the journey!

### How can I help if I can't pledge money?
Share the campaign! Follow us on social media and tell your friends. Beta test features. Submit activity suggestions for your area. Join our community.
