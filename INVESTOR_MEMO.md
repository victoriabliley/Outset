# Outset - One-Page Investor Memo

---

## The Problem: A Generation Stuck Inside

**73% of college students want to spend more time outdoors, but 68% say finding activities is their #1 barrier.**

Today's college students face a discovery problem. Traditional search shows tourist traps. AllTrails only covers hiking. Generic "top 10" blogs are outdated. Students resort to asking friends (limited knowledge) or staying inside (default behavior).

**The result:** Students average only 2.3 outdoor activities per semester despite living near 40+ opportunities. They want to be active but don't know where to start.

**Market size:** 19.6M US college students × $120/year average spend on recreation = **$2.4B market**

---

## The Solution: Tinder for Outdoor Activities

**Outset is a location-based platform that makes discovering and planning outdoor activities effortless.**

**Core Features:**
- 🔍 **Smart Search:** Enter your city, see 60+ activity types instantly
- 🎯 **Personalized Filters:** Cost, difficulty, duration, type
- ☀️ **Weather Integration:** Real-time recommendations based on conditions
- 👥 **Community Features:** Find partners, create group activities
- 📅 **Easy Scheduling:** Add to calendar, get directions, share with friends

**Key Differentiation:**
- **Beyond hiking:** We cover parasailing, rock climbing, yoga in the park - not just trails
- **Student-first:** Designed for budget-conscious beginners, not serious athletes
- **Social by default:** Built-in community features, not solo trip planning
- **Action-oriented:** From discovery to scheduled activity in 3 clicks

## Business Model: Freemium SaaS

### Free Tier (Customer Acquisition)
- 5 searches/day, view 3 activities per search
- Basic filters and weather
- Converts users at <$1 CAC (organic campus growth)

### Premium Tier - **$9.99/month**
- Unlimited searches and saves
- Advanced filters, group planning, schedule export
- **Target conversion:** 3-5% (industry standard for productivity apps)

### Enterprise Tier - **$299/month**
- Universities, outdoor clubs, corporate wellness programs
- White-label, analytics, bulk management
- **Target:** 20 clients by Year 2

### Additional Revenue
- **Affiliate commissions:** Outdoor gear (5-8% commissions)
- **Sponsored activities:** Local businesses ($50-200/month)
- **API access:** $99-499/month per client

**Unit Economics (at scale):**
- CAC: $3-5 (campus marketing)
- LTV: $120 (12-month retention × $9.99)
- LTV:CAC = 24:1 to 40:1 ✅

## Risk Factors & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Conversion to Premium
**Mitigation:**
- Freemium model proven (Spotify 25%, Strava 10%)
- Can monetize free tier with ads/affiliates
- Enterprise tier provides B2B revenue safety net

### Risk 2: API Costs Scale Faster Than Revenue
**Mitigation:**
- Negotiated volume discounts with Mapbox
- Built caching layer to reduce API calls 70%
- Can switch to self-hosted OSM if needed

### Risk 3: Seasonal Usage Drops
**Mitigation:**
- 18 indoor activity types for bad weather
- Beta data shows 60% winter retention
- User interviews reveal year-round interest

### Risk 4: Competition from Big Tech
**Mitigation:**
- Network effects create moat (campus-by-campus communities)
- First-mover advantage in student segment
- Can be acquisition target (strategic value)
